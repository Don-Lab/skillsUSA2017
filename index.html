About Us


Since 1969, Big Brothers Big Sisters has been pairing children, mostly from single-parent homes, with a caring adult friend. It doesn’t take anything special to be a Big Brother or Big Sister. Just hang out for a couple of hours and do the kinds of things you would do with a friend. It could be one of the most exciting and rewarding things you’ll ever do.
